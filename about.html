Our Story

Empowering Dreams Since 2009

Sri Win Education was founded with a singular mission: to bridge the gap between ambitious Sri Lankan students and world-class educational institutions overseas.

Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small consultancy into one of the nation's most trusted education and visa agencies, having successfully placed over 5,000 students across 22 countries.

Our commitment goes beyond paperwork. We provide personalized counseling, visa interview coaching, pre-departure briefings, and post-arrival support — ensuring every student feels confident at every step.
