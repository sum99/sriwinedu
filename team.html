Management Team

Meet the visionary behind Sri Win Lanka Private Limited — a story of entrepreneurship, diversification, and global reach.

2014
Founded Sri Win Lanka
Founder & CEO

Tharindu Nuranga Idunil

Tharindu founded Sri Win Lanka Private Limited in 2014 as a young entrepreneur, launching operations at just 26 years old. He built the company on his firm belief that Sri Lanka holds immense untapped potential in travel and hospitality. His goal was to inject high-level corporate professionalism, structured customer care, and reliable standards into the local tourist transport and guiding industry.

Since inception, he has actively transformed the company from a boutique tour operator into a highly diversified private enterprise with interests spanning tourism, gem exports, MICE travel, global education services, and visa consultancy.

Key Milestones

What He Has Built

From tourism to education — a journey of strategic diversification and relentless growth.

China-Sri Lanka Tourism & Gem Promotion

Positioned the company as a premier bridge between China and Sri Lanka, specializing in unique local travel experiences and native gem exports — including specialized mining tours in Ratnapura.

MICE & Business Travel

Built a massive corporate network facilitating major business travel and outbound MICE tourism. Under his direction, the company annually takes groups of around 200 Sri Lankan entrepreneurs to China's Canton Fair to forge international trade partnerships.

Global Education Services

In December 2023, expanded into higher education by opening a new head office in Hildon Place, Bambalapitiya. Partnering with international bodies, the company now provides student visa processing and consulting for universities in China, the UK, Japan, and Europe.

Global Visa & Fleet Management

Expanded core operations to offer comprehensive business and leisure visa services worldwide, while building an elite in-house transit fleet of modern vehicles (all under five years from manufacturing) managed by licensed, multilingual chauffeur guides.
