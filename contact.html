Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive from prospective students and families.

Typically required documents include: a valid passport, university admission letter, proof of financial capability, academic transcripts, medical clearance, police clearance, and passport-sized photographs. Specific requirements vary by country — our counselors provide a tailored checklist for your destination.

Processing times vary by country. On average: China (2–4 weeks), UK (3–6 weeks), Canada (4–8 weeks), Australia (4–12 weeks), and Schengen countries (2–4 weeks). We monitor your application status and follow up with embassies to expedite whenever possible.

While we cannot guarantee approval (as the final decision rests with the embassy), our documented visa success rate is 98%. We achieve this through meticulous documentation, interview coaching, and pre-screening applicants to ensure they meet all eligibility criteria.

Yes. We have partnerships with universities that offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. We also work with HNB, Commercial Bank, Seylan, and Sarvodaya Bank to facilitate education loans. Our counselors will assess your profile and recommend the best financial pathway.

Our support doesn't end when you board the flight. We provide airport pickup coordination, temporary accommodation arrangements, local SIM guidance, university registration assistance, and a 24/7 helpline for your first month abroad.

Your first consultation is completely free. We believe every student deserves access to expert guidance without upfront barriers. Subsequent counseling sessions for complex cases may carry a nominal fee, which is always disclosed transparently.
